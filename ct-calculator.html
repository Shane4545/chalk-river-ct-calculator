Chalk River WTP · Ontario Chlorine contact (CT) tool

Is there enough chlorine contact time? Ontario, Canada — CT is: how strong the chlorine is × how long the water sits with it in three places: the clearwell, the pipe up to the tower, and the elevated tank. This page estimates parasite and virus disinfection credit using MECP’s CT tables (via the plant workbook), the same steps as the official Chalk River calculator.

Giardia — A tiny parasite that can make people sick; in water it’s often talked about as hardy “cysts.” The Giardia number here is how well chlorine is knocking those down (not a SCADA tag name).
Viruses — A separate score for viruses; same chlorine-contact idea, different rules and targets.

How the math works

This calculator is for an Ontario, Canada plant. The embedded Giardia and virus factors match the CT tables in Ontario’s Procedure for Disinfection of Drinking Water (MECP, adopted under O. Reg. 170/03). Sheet logic is checked against Chalk River CT Calculator.xlsx — Calculations rows 8–10 and 24–27, with Giardia CT and Virus CT as in that workbook. Run scripts/verify_excel_parity.py after workbook edits to re-check.

Symbols (per step)
V = volume (m³) · Q = flow (L/s) · t = time in step (min) · b = baffling (0–1) · G = effective contact (min) · pH, Cl = measured pH and free chlorine (mg/L) for that step · T = water temperature (°C). CEILING(x, s) rounds up to the nearest multiple of s. FLOOR(x, s) rounds down to the nearest multiple of s.

  1. Water volume — clearwell — Full-tank size × level % ÷ 100.
    Vclearwell = Vfull × (level% ÷ 100)
  2. Water volume — pipe — Fixed pipe volume you enter (m³).
    Vpipe = pipe volume (constant)
  3. Water volume — tower — Tower full size × level % ÷ 100, plus optional extra volume (default matches the plant file add-on).
    Vtower = Vtower full × (level% ÷ 100) + Vextra
  4. Flow — clearwell — Higher of the two clearwell-related flows (L/s).
    Qclearwell = max(Qreading 1, Qreading 2)
  5. Flow — pipe — Second clearwell flow only.
    Qpipe = Qreading 2
  6. Flow — tower — Higher of reading 2 and tower outlet flow.
    Qtower = max(Qreading 2, Qtower out)
  7. Time in step (minutes) — Converts m³ and L/s to minutes; zero if volume or flow is not positive.
    t = (V > 0 and Q > 0) ? (V ÷ Q) × (1000 ÷ 60) : 0
  8. Effective contact — Nominal time × baffling. Pipe step uses b = 1.
    G = t × b
  9. pH for Giardia table — Rounded up to 0.5 to pick the strength column (then snapped to the 7–9 table range in software).
    pHceil = CEILING(pH, 0.5)
  10. Chlorine for Giardia k row — Negative chlorine → 0; else round up to 0.2 mg/L steps.
    Clround = (Cl < 0) ? 0 : CEILING(Cl, 0.2)
  11. Giardia k (table path) — Same as Calculations column N: approximate VLOOKUP on rounded chlorine, return the column fixed by CEILING(pH, 0.5). The workbook range is Giardia CT $S$4:$X$18 (0.5 °C k values; pH 7…9 across columns T–X). Water temperature does not change this lookup — it only affects the alternate Giardia path (0.933(T−5)) and virus steps.
    kGiardia = VLOOKUPapprox(Clround, table S:X, col = 2 × pHceil − 12)
  12. Giardia score (one step) — Max of table path and alternate equation (raw pH, Cl, T).
    scoreA = kGiardia × Cl × G
    scoreB = (Cl × G) ÷ ( 0.2828 × pH2.69 × Cl0.15 × 0.933(T − 5) )   (0 if Cl = 0)
    Giardiastep = max(scoreA, scoreB)
  13. Temperature band for viruses — Below 5 °C use 0.5; else floor to 5 °C steps.
    Mvirus = (T < 5) ? 0.5 : FLOOR(T, 5)
  14. Virus table factor — Approximate match on Mvirus; zero if Clround = 0.
    fvirus = (Clround = 0) ? 0 : lookup(Mvirus)
  15. Virus score (one step) — Max of table path and exponential form.
    scoreC = fvirus × Cl × G
    scoreD = (Cl × G × e0.071 × Mvirus − 0.42) ÷ 2.94   (0 if Cl = 0)
    Virusstep = max(scoreC, scoreD)
  16. Which inputs go where — Clearwell and pipe share the same pH, Cl, and T (“clearwell & pipe”). Tower uses tower pH and Cl; T is the same for all three steps.
  17. Total Giardia score — Sum of three steps; display caps at > 3.
    Giardiatotal = Giardiaclearwell + Giardiapipe + Giardiatower
  18. Total virus score — Sum of three steps; display caps at > 4.
    Virustotal = Virusclearwell + Viruspipe + Virustower
  19. Contact × chlorine (achieved) — Sum of Cl × G over steps (same chlorine and G as in each step’s row).
    CTachieved = (Cl × G)1 + (Cl × G)2 + (Cl × G)3
  20. Contact × chlorine (needed) — Scales achieved CT by your target ÷ total log score (when total ≠ 0).
    CTneeded, Giardia = CTachieved × (targetGiardia ÷ Giardiatotal)
    CTneeded, virus = CTachieved × (targetvirus ÷ Virustotal)

Complete formulas (engineers)

Self-contained reference aligned to Calculations rows 8–10 and 24–27. Giardia N matches VLOOKUP(K,'Giardia CT'!$S$4:$X$18,2*I−12) — 0.5 °C table only; P uses raw T in 0.933(T−5). Virus R matches VLOOKUP(M,'Virus CT'!$H$6:$I$11,2) (approximate). Tower volume in Excel uses +300; this page uses Vextra (default 300).

Volumes (m³) and flows (L/s)

V₁ = V_clearwell_full × (level_clearwell% ÷ 100)
V₂ = V_pipe_fixed
V₃ = V_tower_full × (level_tower% ÷ 100) + V_extra

q₁ = max(q_reading1, q_reading2)
q₂ = q_reading2
q₃ = max(q_reading2, q_tower_out)

Per segment i ∈ {1,2,3}

Use (Vᵢ, qᵢ, bᵢ, pHᵢ, Clᵢ) and shared water temperature T (°C).
Clearwell & pipe share pH₁= pH₂, Cl₁ = Cl₂, T. Tower uses pH₃, Cl₃. Pipe baffling b₂ = 1.

tᵢ = (Vᵢ > 0 and qᵢ > 0) ? (Vᵢ ÷ qᵢ) × (1000 ÷ 60) : 0
Gᵢ = tᵢ × bᵢ

Iᵢ = CEILING(pHᵢ, 0.5)
Kᵢ = (Clᵢ < 0) ? 0 : CEILING(Clᵢ, 0.2)

Nᵢ = VLOOKUP_approx(Kᵢ, 'Giardia CT'!S4:X18, 2×Iᵢ − 12)   ← 0.5 °C k matrix; T not used for N

Oᵢ = Nᵢ × Clᵢ × Gᵢ
Pᵢ = (Clᵢ = 0) ? 0 : (Clᵢ × Gᵢ) ÷ ( 0.2828 × pHᵢ^2.69 × Clᵢ^0.15 × 0.933^(T − 5) )
Giardiaᵢ = max(Oᵢ, Pᵢ)

M = (T < 5) ? 0.5 : FLOOR(T, 5)
Rᵢ = (Kᵢ = 0) ? 0 : VirusTable(M)
Sᵢ = Rᵢ × Clᵢ × Gᵢ
Uᵢ = (Clᵢ = 0) ? 0 : (Clᵢ × Gᵢ × e^(0.071 × M) − 0.42) ÷ 2.94
Virusᵢ = max(Sᵢ, Uᵢ)

Plant totals (same as results table)

Giardia_total = Giardia₁ + Giardia₂ + Giardia₃
Virus_total   = Virus₁ + Virus₂ + Virus₃
CT_achieved   = Cl₁×G₁ + Cl₂×G₂ + Cl₃×G₃

CT_needed_Giardia = (Giardia_total ≠ 0) ? CT_achieved × (target_Giardia ÷ Giardia_total) : undefined
CT_needed_virus   = (Virus_total   ≠ 0) ? CT_achieved × (target_virus   ÷ Virus_total)   : undefined

Display: if Giardia_total > 3 show “> 3”; if Virus_total > 4 show “> 4”.
